What is Scalp Ink

What is Scalp Ink?

Scalp Micro Pigmentation (SMP) is an advanced beauty technique originating in Korea. Safety-certified pigment is deposited as micro-dots into the scalp using specialized equipment.

Each session places an average of 30,000–50,000 dots with precision. Hair thinning, shaved-head look, and scars are recreated as a natural hairline — results visible the same day.

For optimal results, we recommend 2–3 sessions based on individual retention rates.

Why Trust Us

3 Reasons to Trust Us

Our scalp experts carefully assess your condition and provide dedicated support through and after treatment.

01
Scalp Knowledge
Scalp Expertise
The scalp is a delicate tissue densely packed with hair follicles, sebaceous glands, and blood vessels. Deep expertise in scalp structure, hair cycles, and thinning patterns enables optimal dot placement, density, and tone design.
  • Needle pressure tuned to follicle depth
  • Natural gradient design along the hairline
  • Long-term design accounting for future thinning progression
02
Counseling & Carte
Thorough Consultation & Records
Before treatment, we conduct a thorough intake using a dedicated consultation chart, reviewing scalp condition, allergies, lifestyle, and design goals in detail to create a fully customized treatment plan.
  • Review of medical history, allergies, and current medications
  • Scalp photos for baseline documentation and progress comparison
  • Proposal and agreement on design, dot count, and number of sessions
  • Thorough discussion of all requests and concerns
03
Post-Care Instructions
Post-Treatment Care Instructions
The scalp is extremely delicate after treatment. To maximize pigment retention and eliminate any risk of uneven color or infection, we carefully explain all post-care instructions.
  • First 72 hours: avoid washing hair, sweating, or getting scalp wet
  • Apply sunscreen to prevent pigment fading
  • Moisturize to maintain scalp condition
  • Post-treatment questions answered anytime via LINE
