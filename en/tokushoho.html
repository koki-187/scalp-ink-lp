Service Details

Service Name Scalp Micro Pigmentation (SMP) Treatment
Service Overview A cosmetic treatment that recreates the natural appearance of hair follicles by applying microscopic dots to the scalp. Korean carbon ink is used, and all treatments are performed by technicians certified by the International Scalp Ink Association (i-SIA).
Treatment Options
  • Hair loss coverage (full scalp or partial)
  • Hairline correction
  • Scar camouflage (surgical or trauma scars)
  • Crown and parting density enhancement
